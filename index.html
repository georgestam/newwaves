About me

I have been always passionate about tech and software, perhaps because I grew up with an ZX Spectrum in the 80s and a Commodore Amiga 500 in the 90s. Despite my love for all things tech, though, I somehow ended up becoming a Mechanical Engineer!

After a successful career spanning over 15 years as an Analyst, Project Manager, Chartered Mechanical Engineer (IMechE), and Lead Subsea Engineer (MSc) in multimillion deep-water projects within Oil & Gas, I decided to make a career change. I turned to web development and enrolled in an intensive 9 week Ruby on Rails program in Le Wagon to become a Full Stack developer.

I now work as a freelancer, building web platforms.

The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now!
